Technical foundation

Modern cryptography, transparent guarantees

SecureShot uses industry-standard cryptography to protect content on device and move access safely between users — without turning the product into a jargon exercise.

Payloads use XChaCha20-Poly1305.
Passphrase and key derivation use Argon2id.
Secure sharing uses Curve25519.

Privacy stance

Private by design

The operator does not receive plaintext media or usable decryption secrets—see Privacy Policy for what sync stores.

We never see your files

Content is encrypted on device; the cloud holds ciphertext, not readable photos or videos.

No plaintext analytics

Product metrics focus on health and usage—not reconstructing your library. Account fields for sync are spelled out in the Privacy Policy.

No content profiling

SecureShot is not built to scrape your media or learn what your files contain.

Zero-knowledge of secrets

Passphrases and keys that decrypt your files are not available to the service—by architecture, matching the hero above.
