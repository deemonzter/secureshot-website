Support

SecureShot encrypts your media into portable .sshot files. For questions about the app, your account, or how encryption and sync work, use the options below.

For general help and technical support, email support@secureshotapp.com. We aim to respond to reasonable inquiries as soon as we can.

For privacy-related requests (including data rights where applicable), contact privacy@secureshotapp.com.

Lost passphrase or keys

SecureShot is designed so that decryption secrets stay on your devices. If you lose passphrases or keys required to unlock your files, we cannot recover that data for you — by design, we do not have those secrets.
