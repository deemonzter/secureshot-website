Introduction

Joseph Daryl Locsin ("we," "us," or "our") operates the SecureShot mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

Our Commitment to Privacy

SecureShot is designed with a zero-knowledge architecture. This means that we cannot access, read, or decrypt any of the files you secure using the App. Your privacy is not just a policy â€” it's built into the core design of our system.

Data Storage and Security

Cloud Storage

Your encrypted files are stored using secure cloud infrastructure (Firebase Storage). These files remain encrypted at all times and cannot be decrypted by us or any third party without your password.

Local Storage

The App stores encrypted files locally in your device's secure sandbox. These files are removed only when you delete the app or manually clear the cache.
